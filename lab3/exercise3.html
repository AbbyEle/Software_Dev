Robots
Robots are automated machines designed to perform tasks traditionally carried out by humans. They can range from simple machines that perform repetitive tasks to complex systems capable of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Robots are used in a variety of fields, including manufacturing, healthcare, and exploration, and have the potential to greatly improve productivity and efficiency, as well as assist in dangerous or precise operations where human intervention may not be safe or effective.
